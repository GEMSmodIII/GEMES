A bit about Year Up would be that it knows how to reach out to people. The people at Year Up knows how to get a job done and how to get people to come and visit and learn more about it. Though we come from relativily similar communities we each heard about this great program a different way. Melissa learned of Year Up from an ad on Creg's List. Genesis along with Elijah heard of the program via word of mouth.

Year Up, in a nutshell is a one year program that focuses on IT and business communication. The program teaches its students about professionalism and how to benefit from the Corporate business life. There have been numberous amounts of graduates from the program that has become succesful in Corporate America. The primary goal of Year Up is to close the opportunity divide of youth who has ledt of a benefit to succeed in Coporate America, and it is not just limited to minorities.
